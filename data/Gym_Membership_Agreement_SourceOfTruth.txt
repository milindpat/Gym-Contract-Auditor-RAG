IRONVAULT FITNESS — MEMBERSHIP AGREEMENT (Source of Truth)
Version 1.0 — Effective: 2026-01-15

1. Membership Plans
1.1 Monthly Plan: Billed monthly until cancelled.
1.2 Annual Plan: Prepaid for 12 months; non-refundable except where required by law.

2. Billing and Payments
2.1 Billing Date: Monthly charges occur on the same calendar day you enroll.
2.2 Late Fees: If payment fails, a late fee of $15 may be added after 7 days.
2.3 Chargebacks: Chargebacks may result in immediate suspension until resolved.

3. Cancellation Policy
3.1 Monthly Plan Cancellation: You may cancel at any time with 7 days notice before your next billing date.
3.2 Cancellation Method: Cancellations must be submitted through the member portal or in person at the front desk.
3.3 Annual Plan Cancellation: Early cancellation does not provide refunds for unused months.

4. Freeze (Pause) Policy
4.1 Freeze Eligibility: Members may freeze their membership for medical reasons or extended travel.
4.2 Freeze Limit: Up to 60 days per calendar year.
4.3 Freeze Fee: A $5 administrative fee applies per freeze request.

5. Guest Policy
5.1 Guests are allowed only during staffed hours.
5.2 Each member may bring up to 2 guests per month.
5.3 Guests must sign a liability waiver upon entry.

6. Facility Rules
6.1 Members must follow staff instructions and posted rules.
6.2 Harassment, vandalism, or unsafe behavior may result in termination.

7. Suspension and Termination
7.1 The gym may suspend or terminate membership for violations of this Agreement or unsafe behavior.
7.2 Termination may be immediate for severe misconduct.

8. Liability and Assumption of Risk
8.1 Fitness activities carry inherent risk. Members assume responsibility for their participation.
8.2 The gym is not responsible for lost or stolen items.

9. Privacy
9.1 The gym collects basic personal information for membership management.
9.2 Do not include sensitive personal data (e.g., SSNs) in messages to staff.

10. Contact
10.1 Billing questions: billing@ironvault.example
10.2 Membership help: support@ironvault.example
